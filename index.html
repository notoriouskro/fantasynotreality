Draft

The Draft will be held on the third weekend from August. The draft will be a live draft and owners need to do whatever they can to make the draft. I understand life happens and in special circumstances we can make changes. If you will not be able to make it, the more notice the better.

The Draft will be held on the third weekend from August. The draft will be a live draft and owners need to do whatever they can to make the draft. I understand life happens and in special circumstances we can make changes. If you will not be able to make it, the more notice the better.

The buy in will be $100 and is due at the draft Payouts are as follows ($600/$200/$200) $100 for most total points

Keepers

Any player drafted in the 7th round or later can be kept the following season assuming you drafted them and they are on your roster at the seasons end.

Each season any player kept, must be taking 2 rounds earlier than their draft spot previous season.

Keepers are due one week before the draft.

Scoring

All touchdowns scored are worth 6 points Every catch is worth 1/2 point

IR

There is an IR spot where any injured player based upon ESPN designation may be placed
